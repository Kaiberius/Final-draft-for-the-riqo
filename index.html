How to use

Create an account or login

To use our web app, you'll need to either create an account or log in if you already have one. To create an account, simply click the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button and follow the prompts to enter your email address, choose a password, and provide any additional information required. To log in, click the "Log In" or "Sign In" button and enter your email address and password. If you forget your password, you can reset it by clicking the "Forgot Password" link.

Tap the generate button

To generate a qoute, simply tap the "Generate" button. This button is typically located below the qoute block, and is easy to identify by its blue color. Once you tap the button, you should see a quote. If you have any issues with the generate button or the generated output, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.

Wait and watch

After you've completed the second step, you'll need to wait a moment. During this time, please do not refresh the page or navigate away from the current screen, as this may interrupt the process. Instead, simply sit back and watch as the generator does its thing. Once the process is complete, the result should be a Quote. If you have any questions or concerns during the wait and watch step, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.
